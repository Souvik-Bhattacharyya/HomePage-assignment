About Us

Welcome To Amrit Somani
Memorial Center

Amrit Somani Memorial Center (ASMC) is a private, nonprofit intervention center at Sarat Bose Road, Kolkata, for children with Autism and other neurodevelopmental disorders.

  • Explore a variety of fresh educational teach.
  • Our aim is to help these children overcome the challenges they face, so that they can be integrated into mainstream schools and the community. 
  • Re-imagined interective video learning.
PROGRAM

Home Based

ASMC is the only place in India that provides home-based services to the people on the spectrum and their parents.

PROGRAM

Center Based

There are four major behavioral interventions and programs that we work on catering to the spectrum of needs of each individual.

PROGRAM

School Based

There are 2 models that have been primarily designed under this category: school consultation and school shadow. 

PROGRAM

Vocation Based

We also provide regular vocational training to individuals who are seeking for employment.

Overview

Home Profile Messages
Our vision is to improve the quality of living for all our students on the autism spectrum. We intend on achieving this through improving and providing communication, language comprehension, play and social skills, academics, self sufficiency and vocational skills. We hope to create tangible and sustainable changes that can be observed in the child’s life even outside of the center.
